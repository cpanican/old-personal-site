Totobitz

Description

This game features a famous street food in the Philippines, fishballs! As a fishball street food vendor, you must serve customers in a fast paced manner. If someone comes to your stall, you must have something cooked beforehand because customers would typically grab a stick and pick for themselves. In order to be successful, you must also provide a small variety of sauces such as regular sauce, spicy sauce, and vinegar. In addition, it is ideal to serve other types of fishballs in your menu such as Kikiam, Squidball, and even mini-hotdogs or kwek-kwek (mini eggs fried in a batter). In this game, as you earn more money, you could upgrade your stall so it could look even fancier! However, keep an eye on your supplies before doing so!
